Quick Questions

How fast will I hear back?

24 hours max. WhatsApp gets faster replies—we check that constantly. Email gets responses within a business day.

Does Indiloom charge fees?

No. Free for buyers. Free for suppliers too, for now. We make money when partnerships succeed—not from listing fees.

Do I need to share my company details?

We need a valid email and phone to match you with suppliers. Company details—keep them confidential if you want. Suppliers will know it eventually if you proceed, but we won't pressure you.

What if I'm just researching?

Research away. Submit an inquiry with your questions. We'll give you market insights, supplier capabilities, pricing ranges—even if you're not ready to order yet.
