Sourcing Home Textiles from Panipat

Real questions, real answers

Why Panipat?

Panipat has 40+ years of textile manufacturing. It's where capacity lives. But more important: it's where knowledge lives. You get competitive prices, consistent quality, and factories that know how to scale without compromising standards.

What about quality standards?

All our suppliers are verified for GST compliance and labor regulations. Many have ISO 9001 or equivalent. We can arrange third-party testing and audits for your specific requirements. Quality is non-negotiable.

How fast is the process?

Requirement to quotes/samples: 7-10 business days. Once you order: 30-90 days depending on complexity and shipping. We don't promise fantasy timelines. These are real numbers.

Can suppliers do custom work?

Yes—most offer custom printing, dyeing, weaves, colors, and sizes. MOQs vary (typically 500-2,000 units). We connect you with suppliers who match your customization needs and budget.

What's typical on payment terms?

Most common: 50% upfront + 50% on shipment. Or 30% deposit with LC (Letter of Credit). Larger volumes = better terms. We help negotiate based on your volume and relationship history.

Can I visit the factories?

Yes. For bigger orders especially. We can coordinate site visits, quality inspections, and meetings with suppliers. Panipat is in Haryana, roughly 2 hours from Delhi airport.
