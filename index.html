Why We Built This

Panipat manufactures 60% of India's home textiles. But that doesn't mean it's easy to find a good supplier. Retailers, exporters, and interior firms spend weeks cold-calling mills, dealing with middlemen, and hoping they don't get scammed.

Factories have their own problem: sporadic demand. One big order one year, nothing the next. Most compete only on price. Quality gets ignored.

We believed there had to be a better way. Connect serious buyers directly with serious suppliers. Cut the BS. Let quality and reliability be the differentiator, not who has the best salesman.
